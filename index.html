Our Work Process

How To Get Started

We keep the process simple: clear scope, clear pricing, and a clean finish. Here’s how a typical Serene Landscaping project runs.

  • Scope and Site Check

    We review your goals, measure the space, and flag grading, drainage, access, and material considerations.

  • Quote and Plan

    You get a clear quote and timeline. If needed, we outline base depth, materials, and finish details before we start.

  • Build and Updates

    We complete the work cleanly and safely, and keep you updated on progress, changes, and what to expect next.

  • Final Walkthrough

    We walk the project with you, confirm the details, and leave the site tidy with care tips for sod, plants, or pavers.
